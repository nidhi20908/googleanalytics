Our Story

Food as a
living memory

Ember & Salt was born from a simple obsession: what happens when traditional coastal cuisine meets the precision of contemporary technique? Chef Anika Sharma returned from years in the Basque country with an answer.

Every dish begins at the source — dawn market visits, relationships with local farmers, and a kitchen where nothing is wasted. The charcoal grill and the cold brine define us.
